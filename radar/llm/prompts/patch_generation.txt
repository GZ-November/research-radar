Generate the smallest possible manuscript edit using only confirmed impacts and supplied citations.

Do not change locked numerical values.

Choose the target from the confirmed impact, not merely from the claim anchor:
- prior_art / cite: edit positioning in Introduction, Related Work, or Discussion. Never attach
  the citation to an empirical result sentence unless the incoming paper directly supports that
  exact result under compatible Task/Dataset/Split/Metric/Comparator/Scope conditions.
- boundary_condition: edit Discussion or Limitations and state the exact setting difference.
- replication with compatible conditions: qualify the claim and recommend reproduction or team review.
- method_substitution / run_comparison: add an experiment TODO or comparison-plan sentence; do not
  claim the new method wins before the comparison is run.
- research_integrity: mark the affected evidence/result for revalidation.

reference index such as [28]. The application will resolve [CITATION] from the supplied source record.
For partial, incompatible, or unknown comparability, do not rewrite the original numeric result as
false; add a boundary, positioning, or next-step statement instead.
